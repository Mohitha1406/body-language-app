How It Works

From Video to Insights
in Seconds

Six simple steps powered by MediaPipe AI and Computer Vision technology

01
📹

Record Video

Open the app and record a short 10-second presentation video using your phone camera.

02
🦴

Pose Detection

MediaPipe AI detects 33 body keypoints — head, shoulders, spine, arms, and hands.

03
📊

Pattern Analysis

Algorithms analyze movement frequency, posture stability, and gesture patterns.

04
🎯

Score Generated

A Confidence Score (0–100%) is calculated based on detected behavioral patterns.

05
💡

Personalized Tips

Specific, actionable suggestions based on your individual movement patterns.

06
📈

Track Progress

Session history saves your scores so you can see improvement over time.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026 · Body Language AI · Developed by Papudesi Mohitha

1. Information We Collect

Body Language AI may collect video recordings you create within the app solely for the purpose of body language analysis. We may also collect basic usage data such as session timestamps and confidence scores to enable the history and progress tracking features.

2. How We Use Your Information

Video data is processed locally on your device or sent to our analysis server only for the purpose of generating a confidence score and feedback. We do not use your video data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the analysis service.

3. Data Storage

Session history data (scores, timestamps, feedback) may be stored using Firebase Firestore associated with your account. Video files are not permanently stored on our servers after analysis is complete.

4. Camera Permission

The app requires camera permission solely to record video for body language analysis. We do not access your camera at any time other than when you actively initiate a recording session within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses Firebase (by Google) for data storage and authentication. Firebase's privacy policy applies to data processed through their services. MediaPipe by Google is used for AI pose estimation processing.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact the developer: Papudesi Mohitha · B.Tech AI & DS · Saveetha Engineering College, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
